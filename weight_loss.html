Breakfast

Scrambled Tofu Recipe:

1. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.

2. Add diced tofu and cook until lightly browned.

3. Season with salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs.

4. Serve hot.

Total Calories: 150 kcal

Avocado Toast Recipe:

1. Toast bread until golden brown.

2. Mash avocado and spread it on the toast.

3. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes on top.

4. Drizzle olive oil and serve immediately.

Total Calories: 200 kcal

Oatmeal Recipe:

1. Bring water or milk to a boil in a saucepan.

2. Stir in oats and reduce heat to medium.

3. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

4. Remove from heat and let it sit for 2 minutes before serving.

Total Calories: 220 kcal

Chickpea Salad Recipe:

1. Mix cooked chickpeas, diced vegetables, and a dressing of your choice.

2. Toss well and refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.

3. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve chilled.

Total Calories: 180 kcal

Lunch

Quinoa Salad Recipe:

1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions and let it cool.

2. Mix cooked quinoa with chopped vegetables and a dressing of your choice.

3. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.

Total Calories: 250 kcal

Bean Burrito Recipe:

1. Warm tortillas in a skillet or microwave.

2. Fill tortillas with cooked beans, rice, salsa, and guacamole.

3. Roll up and serve with a side of salad.

Total Calories: 320 kcal

Greek Yogurt Recipe:

1. Strain yogurt using a cheesecloth to remove excess liquid.

2. Mix in honey or fruit for sweetness.

3. Refrigerate for a few hours before serving.

Total Calories: 100 kcal

Protein Shake Smoothie Recipe:

1. Blend protein powder, banana, and almond milk until smooth.

2. Add ice cubes for a colder consistency if desired.

3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

Total Calories: 300 kcal

Snacks

Fruit Salad Recipe:

1. Chop assorted fruits such as apples, oranges, and berries.

2. Toss together with a squeeze of lemon juice.

3. Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.

Total Calories: 150 kcal

Brown Rice Recipe:

1. Rinse brown rice under cold water.

2. In a pot, bring water to a boil and add rice.

3. Cover and simmer for 45 minutes or until rice is tender.

4. Fluff with a fork before serving.

Total Calories: 112 kcal

Dinner

Veggie Stir-Fry Recipe:

1. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped vegetables.

2. Stir-fry until vegetables are tender yet crisp.

3. Add soy sauce and seasonings of your choice.

4. Serve hot with cooked rice or noodles.

Total Calories: 280 kcal

Vegetable Soup Recipe:

1. Sauté onions, garlic, and vegetables in a pot until tender.

2. Add vegetable broth and simmer for 20 minutes.

3. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs to taste.

4. Serve hot with crusty bread.

Total Calories: 180 kcal
