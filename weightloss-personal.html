Monday: High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

1. Exercise: Jumping Jacks

Instructions: Perform jumping jacks at a rapid pace, ensuring full extension of arms and legs with each jump.

Duration: 20 minutes

Calories Burned: Approximately 200-300 calories

2. Exercise: Burpees

Instructions: Start in a standing position, squat down, and place your hands on the floor. Jump your feet back into a plank position, perform a push-up, jump your feet back to the squat position, and explosively jump up with your arms overhead.

Sets: 3 sets

Reps: 10-15 reps per set

Calories Burned: Approximately 250-350 calories

Tuesday: Strength Training with Circuit Workout

1. Exercise: Squats

Instructions: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, squat down by bending your knees and pushing your hips back as if you're sitting in a chair. Keep your chest up and back straight, then return to the standing position.

Sets: 3 sets

Reps: 12-15 reps per set

Calories Burned: Approximately 100-150 calories

2. Exercise: Push-Ups

Instructions: Start in a plank position with hands shoulder-width apart. Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor, then push back up to the starting position.

Sets: 3 sets

Reps: 8-12 reps per set

Calories Burned: Approximately 50-100 calories

3. Exercise: Lunges

Instructions: Stand with feet together, step forward with one leg and lower your hips until both knees are bent at about a 90-degree angle. Push back up to the starting position and repeat with the other leg.

Sets: 3 sets

Reps: 10-12 reps per leg per set

Calories Burned: Approximately 100-150 calories

Wednesday: Cardiovascular Exercise - Running

1. Exercise: Running

Instructions: Start with a brisk warm-up walk for 5 minutes. Then, jog at a moderate pace for 20-30 minutes, aiming to cover as much distance as possible within the time frame. Cool down with a 5-minute walk.

Duration: 30-40 minutes

Calories Burned: Approximately 300-400 calories

Thursday: Core Strengthening - Pilates

1. Exercise: Pilates Routine

Instructions: Follow a structured Pilates routine focusing on core engagement and controlled movements. Include exercises such as the Hundred, Roll-Up, Leg Circles, Plank, etc.

Duration: 30 minutes

Calories Burned: Approximately 150-250 calories

Friday: Active Rest Day - Walking

1. Exercise: Brisk Walking

Instructions: Take a brisk walk outdoors or on a treadmill at a pace where you can maintain a conversation but still feel slightly breathless. Focus on good posture and swinging your arms naturally.

Duration: 45-60 minutes

Calories Burned: Approximately 200-300 calories

Saturday: Full Body Cardio - Cycling

1. Exercise: Cycling

Instructions: Start with a 5-minute warm-up at a comfortable pace. Then, increase the intensity and cycle at a moderate to high intensity for 30-45 minutes, varying the resistance if on a stationary bike. Finish with a 5-minute cool-down.

Duration: 40-55 minutes

Calories Burned: Approximately 400-500 calories

Sunday: Rest and Recovery - Gentle Stretching

1. Exercise: Full Body Stretch

Instructions: Begin in a standing position and stretch your arms overhead, reaching towards the ceiling. Hold the stretch for 15-30 seconds, feeling the extension through your entire body. Release and repeat for 2-3 times.

Duration: 5 minutes

2. Exercise: Hamstring Stretch

Instructions: Sit on the floor with one leg extended straight in front of you and the other leg bent with the sole of the foot against the inner thigh of the extended leg. Reach forward towards your toes, keeping your back straight. Hold for 15-30 seconds, then switch legs.

Duration: 2-3 minutes (30 seconds per leg)

3. Exercise: Child's Pose

Instructions: Start on your hands and knees, then sit back onto your heels while reaching your arms forward on the floor. Lower your chest towards the ground and relax your forehead to the floor. Hold this position, focusing on deep breathing and relaxing the muscles of your back and shoulders.

Duration: 2-3 minutes

4. Exercise: Seated Spinal Twist

Instructions: Sit on the floor with your legs extended in front of you. Bend your right knee and cross it over your left leg, placing your right foot flat on the floor outside your left knee. Twist your torso to the right, placing your left elbow on the outside of your right knee. Hold the twist for 15-30 seconds, then switch sides.

Duration: 2-3 minutes (30 seconds per side)

5. Exercise: Relaxation Meditation

Instructions: Find a comfortable seated or lying position. Close your eyes and focus on your breath. Inhale deeply through your nose, filling your lungs with air, and exhale slowly through your mouth, releasing any tension in your body. Continue this deep breathing pattern for 5-10 minutes, allowing yourself to relax fully.
