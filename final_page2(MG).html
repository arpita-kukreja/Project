Monday: Strength Training - Upper Body Focus

1. Exercise: Bench Press

Instructions: Lie on a flat bench with a barbell, grip slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Lower the barbell to your chest, then press it back up to full extension.

Sets: 4 sets

Reps: 8-10 reps per set

Rest: 90 seconds between sets

Calories Burned: Approximately 100-150 calories

2. Exercise: Bent-Over Rows

Instructions: Stand with a barbell in front of you, grip shoulder-width apart. Bend your knees slightly, hinge at the hips, and lean forward. Pull the barbell towards your lower chest, then lower it back down with control.

Sets: 4 sets

Reps: 8-12 reps per set

Rest: 90 seconds between sets

Calories Burned: Approximately 100-150 calories

3. Exercise: Overhead Shoulder Press

Instructions: Stand with feet hip-width apart, hold a pair of dumbbells at shoulder height with palms facing forward. Press the dumbbells overhead until your arms are fully extended, then lower back down to shoulder height.

Sets: 3 sets

Reps: 10-12 reps per set

Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets

Calories Burned: Approximately 75-100 calories

Tuesday: Strength Training - Lower Body Focus

1. Exercise: Deadlifts

Instructions: Stand with feet hip-width apart, barbell over mid-foot. Bend at hips and knees, grip the bar with hands shoulder-width apart. Keep back flat, lift the bar by extending hips and knees. Stand tall with shoulders back and chest up, then return the bar to the floor.

Sets: 4 sets

Reps: 6-8 reps per set

Rest: 90 seconds between sets

Calories Burned: Approximately 150-200 calories

2. Exercise: Romanian Deadlifts

Instructions: Stand with feet hip-width apart, hold a barbell in front of thighs with an overhand grip. Hinge at hips, keep knees slightly bent, lower the barbell towards the floor while keeping back straight. Squeeze glutes to return to standing position.

Sets: 3 sets

Reps: 8-10 reps per set

Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets

Calories Burned: Approximately 100-150 calories

3. Exercise: Bulgarian Split Squats

Instructions: Stand facing away from a bench, place one foot behind you on the bench. Lower your body until your front thigh is parallel to the ground, then push through your front heel to return to the starting position.

Sets: 3 sets

Reps: 10-12 reps per leg per set

Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets

Calories Burned: Approximately 100-150 calories

Wednesday: Cardiovascular Exercise - Swimming

1. Exercise: Swimming

Instructions: Start with a warm-up of freestyle or breaststroke for 5-10 minutes. Then, perform intervals of different strokes (freestyle, backstroke, butterfly) at high intensity for 1-2 minutes followed by 1-2 minutes of easy swimming for recovery.

Duration: 30-45 minutes

Calories Burned: Approximately 300-400 calories

3. Exercise: Russian Twists

Instructions: Sit on the floor with knees bent and feet elevated slightly off the ground. Hold a weight or medicine ball with both hands, rotate your torso to one side, then to the other side, tapping the weight on the floor each time.

Sets: 3 sets

Reps: 15-20 reps per set

Rest: 30-60 seconds between sets

Calories Burned: Approximately 50-75 calories

Thursday: Strength Training - Full Body Circuit

1. Exercise: Pull-Ups/Chin-Ups

Instructions: Grip a pull-up bar with palms facing away (pull-ups) or palms facing towards you (chin-ups). Pull your body up until your chin clears the bar, then lower back down with control.

Sets: 3 sets

Reps: 6-10 reps per set

Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets

Calories Burned: Approximately 75-150 calories

2. Exercise: Dumbbell Lunges with Bicep Curls

Instructions: Hold a pair of dumbbells at your sides, step forward into a lunge with one leg while simultaneously performing a bicep curl with both arms. Return to the starting position and repeat on the other leg.

Sets: 3 sets

Reps: 10-12 reps per leg per set

Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets

Calories Burned: Approximately 100-150 calories

Friday: Active Rest Day - Yoga

1. Exercise: Yin Yoga

Instructions: In Yin Yoga, hold passive, seated postures for longer durations (typically 3-5 minutes or more). Focus on relaxation and stretching deep connective tissues.

Duration: 60-75 minutes

Calories Burned: Approximately 150-200 calories

Sunday: Rest and Recovery - Stretching and Mobility Work

1. Exercise: Full Body Stretch Routine

Instructions: Perform a series of stretches targeting major muscle groups, focusing on holding each stretch for 15-30 seconds and breathing deeply.

Duration: 15-20 minutes

Calories Burned: Negligible
