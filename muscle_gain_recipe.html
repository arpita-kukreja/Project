Breakfast

High-Protein Pancakes Recipe:

1. Mix protein powder, oats, egg whites, and milk in a bowl.

2. Heat a non-stick pan and pour the batter to make pancakes.

3. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.

4. Serve with Greek yogurt and berries.

Total Calories: 350 kcal

Egg White Omelette Recipe:

1. Whisk egg whites with spinach, tomatoes, and bell peppers.

2. Pour the mixture into a heated skillet and cook until set.

3. Fold the omelette and serve with whole-grain toast.

Total Calories: 250 kcal

Greek Yogurt with Almonds and Honey Recipe:

1. Spoon Greek yogurt into a bowl.

2. Top with sliced almonds and a drizzle of honey.

3. Serve chilled.

Total Calories: 250 kcal

Lunch

Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Recipe:

1. Marinate chicken breasts with olive oil, garlic, and herbs.

2. Grill until cooked through and serve with roasted sweet potatoes.

3. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.

Total Calories: 400 kcal

Snack

Protein-Packed Nut Mix Recipe:

1. Mix almonds, cashews, hazelnuts, and walnuts in a bowl.

2. Add a sprinkle of sea salt and toss well.

3. Portion into small bags for easy snacking.

Total Calories: 200 kcal

Dinner

Salmon with Quinoa Recipe:

1. Season salmon fillets with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.

2. Bake in the oven until fish flakes easily with a fork.

3. Serve over cooked quinoa and steamed vegetables.

Total Calories: 350 kcal

Tofu Stir-Fry Recipe:

1. Press tofu to remove excess moisture and cut into cubes.

2. Stir-fry tofu with mixed vegetables and soy sauce.

3. Serve over brown rice or quinoa.

Total Calories: 300 kcal
